# Rounds
- Preliminary Round: Conducted at individual schools (mid-June to early July).
- Zonal Final: Held at regional centers in August.
- Grand Finale: Hosted at NIT Calicut in September.

# Eligibility
- Open to students of Classes 7 to 12.
- Junior Category: Classes 7 to 10.
- Senior Category: Classes 11 to 12.
- Teams of 2 students.
